Unhelpful Visualization

There are two major pain points with this graph that make it confusing for me to understand. The first of which being dates of events being in the incorrect column. In the middle column labeled 1981-2000, two of the major events pointed out are labeled as taking place during 2007 & 2009. This makes it confusing what date range the column actually covers. The second thing that makes this visualization confusing is the direction of the Y-axis. Values on the Y-axis decrease as you move to a greater vertial height and while for many graphs this might be ok, given that this graph is focused on the space above Earth, this decision makes it harder to understand at a glance.
